INTRODUCING EXPANSIOR

Expansior is a network marketing organisation established to address human needs in meaningful ways. We enable people to achieve personal goals and dreams while contributing MASSIVELY to social and economic change.

Our VISION is to build a tremendously successful group of companies with a global foot-print, serving the South African nation in distinctive, significant, life changing ways that will accelerate social and economic development.

We believe in the power of education to transform society, reduce inequality and eradicate poverty. For this reason, our first mission is to address access to higher education. We do this by marketing and raising funds for iGen Education Trust. www.igenedutrust.org.za

While the core of our business activities rests in South Africa, we plan to have considerable presence in the rest of Africa, followed by markets in Asia, the Americas, Caribbean Islands, Europe and Australia. Our desire to spread our wings to global markets is driven by the increasing poverty and growing inequality among members of society across the world.

We are fully persuaded that by harnessing the power of network marketing, we can impact destinies. EXPANSIOR IS NOT JUST A BUSINESS, WE ARE NATION BUILDERS.

MEET THE TEAM


Nosipho Yedwa, Founder and CEO

Nosipho is a UCT graduate and has qualifications from the likes of the AAA School of Advertising and the University of Stellenbosch Business School.

With a Marketing, Brand and Stakeholder Relations career spanning close to two decades, Nosipho has held various senior roles in Marketing and Business Development, including Group Head of Marketing and National Marketing Manager for JSE-listed corporations in the Financial Services sector. Her achievements include introducing a new product which increased her employer’s revenue by well over 10%, successfully spearheading the re-positioning of a brand with marked results on the bottom line, building and managing a productive network of brokers which directly resulted in a 28% growth in new business.

A purpose-driven person known for her commitment to excellence, she is a visionary leader and strategic thinker. Her solution-focused mindset and deep desire to impact lives meaningfully are the foundation upon which Expansior was founded.


Justice Matshili, Founder and Director: International Business

Justice is a scientist from the University of Fort Hare. Prior to leaving formal employment, he was Acting Chief Director: Fisheries Research and Development, Department of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries. He’s solutions oriented. His glowing accolades include being part of a National Committee which crafted the Occupation Specific Dispensation (OSD) for scientists and engineers which solved career challenges faced by scientists and engineers in South Africa. He was part of a committee which developed the policy behind the sea-going allowance used to solve challenges related to extensive overtime by sea-going personnel in South Africa.

Justice is passionate about solving complex societal challenges using simple but practical and realistic solutions.
